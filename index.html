Welcome, traveler.

U.N.M is a player-driven survival experience where your choices actually matter. Whether you want to amass wealth, master a trade, or rise to the rank of Noble, your legacy is in your hands. Choose your path as a Merchant, an Adventurer, a Vendor, or take on roles unique to each kingdom.

Our world runs on a dynamic economy. Earn your fortune through your profession, buy and sell at the server shop or with other players, and test your wealth in daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly bidding auctions.

But tread carefully: actions have consequences. Each kingdom governs its own local laws, but violating global server rules will land you in the U.N.M Court — trials held in public, for the whole community to witness.

Kingdom Of Olise

Seat of the Royal Academy — First Among All Realms

The Kingdom Of Olise is the undisputed economic powerhouse of the server. No other kingdom comes close in wealth, reach, or opportunity — if you are looking to build a fortune, earn a name, or advance through the ranks faster than anywhere else, Olise is where that happens. At its heart stands the Royal Academy, the largest and most prestigious institution on the server. Merchants, scholars, and ambitious newcomers all arrive here first, and many never leave.

Kryden Empire

The Empire That Holds Its Ground

The Kryden Empire sits well behind the Kingdom Of Olise — the gap is wide, and closing it is a long road. That said, Kryden is far from a lost cause. The Empire has working infrastructure, a loyal and active player base, and a growing identity that sets it apart from simply being "the other one." It's a solid place to settle if you prefer a less pressured start, a tighter community feel, and room to grow without competing at Olise's pace from day one.

Oblivian Regnum

Beyond This Point — You Are Not Welcome

The Oblivian Regnum is the third kingdom on the server. It is also the last thing you will be told about it. Its borders are closed. No player is permitted inside. The Regnum enforces its isolation without negotiation — attempts to cross its boundary are handled swiftly. What exists within its walls, who governs it, and why it remains sealed are not questions with public answers. This is not a kingdom you join. It is a kingdom that watches.
